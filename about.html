About FixHub

FixHub is a student-built Ethiopian marketplace that connects customers with trusted sellers and technicians. It makes shopping, booking, and local service discovery simple, secure, and professional, with delivery support built into the experience.

What FixHub does

FixHub helps people browse products, find technicians, book services, and manage secure purchases in one place. It was created as a practical student project to combine ecommerce and repair/service support.

Why it matters

The platform focuses on trust, convenience, and easy communication between customers, sellers, and service providers. It is designed to support local business visibility and smooth online interactions.

Our goal

We aim to build a clean, modern marketplace that feels professional, friendly, and useful for everyday users in Ethiopia.
